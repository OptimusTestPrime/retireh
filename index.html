Methodology

This tool calculates the "Gross-Up" required to hit your net target. It accounts for early retirement penalties and inflation (2.2%).

Penalty Rules

  • Federal: 5% per year short of age 60 (G1) or 65 (G2) unless 30 years service met.
  • Provincial: 3% per year short of 85 points or age 65.
