Assumptions & Methodology

This tool calculates the "Gross-Up" required to hit your net target based on specified economic factors and pension rules.

Economic Logic

  • Growth & Inflation: These rates are specified by the user. Inflation indices your target income goal and government benefits annually.
  • CPP & OAS: Combined estimate of ~$21,000/year (today's dollars) starting at age 65.

Pension Rules

  • Federal: 5% penalty per year short of age 60 (G1) or 65 (G2) unless 30 years service met. A bridge benefit is paid until age 65.
  • Provincial: 3% penalty per year short of 85 points or age 65.

Taxation

  • Strategy: A simplified tax model grossing up withdrawals to ensure the "Net" take-home goal is met across all years.
